About FASL

Football Academy of Sierra Leone (FASL) is the premier football development organization in Sierra Leone, dedicated to nurturing talent and promoting excellence in football.

General Information

The Football Academy of Sierra Leone is the governing body of football in Sierra Leone, affiliated with international football organizations. It oversees the national league, youth development programs, and the national football teams.

Our mission is to develop football talent from grassroots to professional level, promoting the sport as a tool for youth empowerment and national development. We believe in creating opportunities for young athletes to reach their full potential both on and off the field.

Agency History

Founded in 2018, FASL has grown to become the leading football development institution in Sierra Leone. Starting with just 50 young players, we now train over 500 athletes annually across various age groups and skill levels.

Our journey began with a vision to transform football development in Sierra Leone. Through strategic partnerships and dedicated coaching, we have established a comprehensive development pathway that has produced players for both local and international competitions.

Agency Achievements

  • 2024: National U-20 Championship winners
  • 2023: Three players selected for the Leone Stars national team
  • 2022: Partnership established with European football academies
  • 2021: FASL league expanded to include women's division
  • 2020: Construction of new training facility completed
  • 2019: First international youth tournament participation
  • 2018: FASL officially launched with inaugural youth program
